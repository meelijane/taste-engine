Big-tech apps learn what you like and decide what you see next; you rarely find out why, and you never really learn anything about your own taste. Taste Engine flips that. Pick the things you love and it helps you put words to your taste — the styles and through-lines you keep coming back to, in plain language. The point is to understand yourself a little better and find new things you'll genuinely like, on your own terms instead of an algorithm's.
