Taste Engine

What do you actually like?

Pick the things you love. Get the read on yourself — in words, on a map, and as a pile of stuff worth going to find.

The idea

Learn your taste. Choose what you explore.

Most apps learn what you like, then quietly decide what you see next — and the reasons stay hidden from you. Taste Engine is built to put you back in charge of that. Pick the things you genuinely love, and it shows you the shape of your taste: the styles and through-lines you keep returning to, named in plain language. From there, discovery is a choice you make, not a feed that makes it for you. The goal is simple — rely a little less on algorithms you can't see, and a little more on your own judgment about what's worth your time.

A vocabulary for taste

Put language to what you actually love — the styles, the textures, the through-lines you keep coming back to.

Exploration with agency

Discover by choice, not by autoplay. Nothing gets silently slipped into a feed on your behalf.

Recommendations you opt into

In the spirit of Last.fm and Pandora: suggestions you invite, not curation that decides for you.

More human judgment in the loop. Fewer black boxes.
